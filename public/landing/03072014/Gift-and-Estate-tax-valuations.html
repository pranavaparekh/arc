The dynamics of Gift and Estate Tax regulations have changed significantly over the years. As you evaluate your wealth transition planning options, please bear in mind that IRS is now watching closely. In this specter of heightened scrutiny, there are no shortcuts when it comes to valuations, which need to be more than just Boilerplate reports. In the face of evolving tax regulations, in-depth understanding of business and the nuances affecting estate tax valuations can mean the difference between money gained and money lost; between success and failure in litigation.
