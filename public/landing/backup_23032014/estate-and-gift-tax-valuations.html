Robust Approach + Qualified Team = Defensible Valuations

The dynamics of Estate & Gift tax valuations have changed significantly over the last 15 years due to intense scrutiny by IRS. About 60% of valuations of over USD5 million and almost 100% of estates over USD10 million are examined 100% by IRS. Therefore, there are no short cuts to diligent valuations.

Understanding the nuances of business as well as ever-changing and evolving tax regulations affecting estate tax valuations can mean the difference between money gained and money lost; and between success and failure in litigation.
