Why Prior Art Search?

With nearly five million patents and trademarks being filed annually, patents as a strategic resource has gained significance over the last two decades. As a result, both patent owners and innovators have a lot at stake when it comes to defining R&D focus, planning international product launches and managing or protecting inventions. Knowing whether a patent is novel or has incremental improvements on existing patents with reasonable degree of confidence, can only determine whether a patent should be filed. In that sense, prior art search is professional task that can only be executed if right tools, strategies and databases are at hand. Aranca’s prior art searches help you make the right decisions before deciding the course of action and investments.
