Versatility and Depth

Aranca’s credit & fixed income analysts leverage years of experience and market expertise to create carefully tailored research & analytics to our clients. They track market and regulatory changes, current market dynamics and economic outlooks to deliver strategies that enable clients to get better returns.

We support clients in credit modeling, initiating coverage, developing investment-grade and high-yield ideas, structured products, maintenance research, preparing and maintaining relative value databases, conducting analysis, portfolio tracking and several other activities. Since inception, we have worked with several fixed income sell-side firms, for whom we have prepared credit models and notes (initiation and updates) on over 400 companies. For buy-side, we have tracked 1000+ securities and maintained relative value database on 750 HY securities in the US.
