Challenges Faced

Credit analysts spend almost 40–50% of their time on model updates, financial spreading, and secondary research. This eats into time that could have otherwise been spent ideating new investment ideas or meeting the management of companies under coverage. Growth is extremely difficult in this chaotic environment—so also, chasing alpha!

Many managers are leveraging third-party outsourced fixed income and credit research support for standardized research activities such as financial model updates/maintenance, financial spreading, and daily news support. This makes it easier for managers to both ideate and maintain their portfolio universe more effectively; outsourcing also reduces the load on in-house analysts-experts, freeing them up for front-end work.

Aranca’s credit & fixed income analysts leverage years of experience and market expertise to create carefully tailored research & analytics to our clients. They track market and regulatory changes, current market dynamics and economic outlooks to deliver strategies that enable clients to get better returns. We have:

  • Strong expertise and experience in credit & fixed income markets
  • Depth and breadth of research experience across all major markets
  • Robust processes to drive high quality and actionable research output
  • Cost effective and rapid turnaround

We support clients in credit modeling, initiating coverage, developing investment-grade and high-yield ideas, structured products, maintenance research, preparing and maintaining relative value databases, conducting analysis, portfolio tracking and several other activities. Since inception, we have worked with several fixed income sell-side firms, for whom we have prepared credit models and notes (initiation and updates) on over 400 companies. For buy-side, we have tracked 1000+ securities and maintained relative value database on 750 HY securities in the US.
