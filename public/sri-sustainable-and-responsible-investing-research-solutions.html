Aranca has worked with several SRI focused asset managers in Europe and the US, helping them screen socially responsible companies or identify firms with superior ESG practices relative to their industry peers. By assuming the “heavy lifting” research work, we enable our clients to focus on high-value adding activities like idea generation and fund raising.

Our range of services include:

  • Negative Screening: Creating and maintaining database on SRI compliant companies to include / exclude relevant companies
  • ESG Indicators Analysis: In-depth screening of companies on various ESG criteria that help to rank and select ‘sustainability’ winners
  • Primary Research: Interviews / survey with various stakeholders including discussions with management representatives
