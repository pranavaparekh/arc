Aranca is one of the leading valuation and advisory services provider. We have performed hundreds of stock options valuations for ASC 718 (formerly, FAS 123R) for private companies with their complex capital structures, across diverse industry sectors and enterprise development stages.

Our valuations are thoroughly documented and customized to meet all client-specific parameters which can impact fair value of the instruments.
