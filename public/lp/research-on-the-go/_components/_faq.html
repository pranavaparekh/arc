What is Research-On-The-Go?

  • Aranca Research-On-The-Go is a customizable, modular cloud based platform that delivers research and analysis-backed intelligence solutions that are critical to enable decision-making across business functions in an enterprise.
  • It is an effective combination of technology enabled front-end and expert-backed data validation in the back-end, as an integrated, on-demand service platform.
  • The core value propositions of the Aranca Research-On-The-Go are scalability to address the needs of all the business functions, flexibility to meet dynamic research requirements, faster turn-around -times to meet time critical decision related timelines, enterprise-wide access to the intelligence while breaking silos, and consolidated and optimized research platform at economical and predictable investment.

Why do I need Research-On-The-Go?

  • With Aranca Research-On-The-Go, organizations are at a sweet spot to successfully leverage a reimagined research and analytics solution. A space that effectively marries conventional research service models with user friendly technology solutions to serve the evolving business decision-making needs. And, change the way enterprises consume research.
  • It helps your organization’s busy executives find quick answers to a wide range of simple or complex questions that enable better and timely decision-making for them.
  • With Aranca Research-On-The-Go, you need to just focus on what you do best—take business calls and make informed decisions, and let us handle your research and intelligence needs.
