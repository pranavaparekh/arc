COMMERCIAL AND INDUSTRIAL LENDING

To enable clients to spend more time on sales activities, Aranca partners with clients to provide solutions on some of the pain areas like Market and Industry Studies, Loan Summaries and Periodic Reviews and Recommendations on Preliminary Borrower Rating.

Solution Framework

Aranca C&I teams are working with clients on Market Research and Industry Reviews, Quarterly and Annual Loan Reviews and Appraisal Notes, Preliminary Borrower Rating models, Asset Quality Reviews, Covenant Compliance Reports, Portfolio Monitoring and Collateral Audit Reports.

Aranca Value-Add

  • By streamlining documentation of loan adherence records and periodic updates, our clients get early warning on non-compliant loans.
  • Better cash flow management and floor planning through collateral audit reports
  • Reduced lead time and winning more deals through Market research and Industry reviews

COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE LENDING

Aranca provide support in Multifamily Mortgage Lending, Commercial Mortgage Production through CMBS and direct lending, Healthcare Real Estate, Institutional REITs & Funds, Loan Servicing & Asset Management and Affordable Housing

Solution Framework

Aranca solution products include a) Asset and Cash flow summary reports comprising Market Summary, Loan Summary, Property Summary, Roll-over analysis, SWOT analysis b) Loan origination reports consisting of Property Operating Performance, Tenant analysis, borrower analysis and loan analysis c) Incorporating and analyzing property operating statements and performing re-underwriting based on cash-flow analysis d) testing covenant compliance

Aranca Value-Add

Client’s loan origination team benefits from:

  • More number of deals underwritten with short turnaround time due to Aranca support on research on deal origination
  • Periodic portfolio monitoring and stress testing had helped CRE teams in identifying red-flag in advance for non-compliant portfolios
  • Added benefits of standardization of research and reporting processes

EQUIPMENT LENDING

Clients use Aranca equipment lending services in origination, risk assessment and management and portfolio monitoring. Aranca brings the knowledge of sector expertise and know how in the equipment lending to produce premium asset summary notes.

Solution Framework

Aranca equipment lending teams help on-shore teams in Market studies, Sector reports, Loan appraisals, Annual review notes/ Portfolio monitoring, Preliminary risk ratings, Assessment of change in risk profiles and future implications, Asset summary report, peer benchmarking

Aranca team have experience in equipment lending across sectors including Medical equipment, Computers and software, Construction, agriculture, mining and oilfield, railroad, industrial and materials handling equipment.

Aranca Value-Add

  • Increased efficiency in the underwriting process with a centralized fundamental analysis for the credit team
  • Increased coverage and periodicity of loans monitored by the portfolio management team
  • Developed and maintained early warning systems to consistently monitor loan portfolio for timely actions on deteriorating loans, concentration risk, event risk, etc
  • Filtered and processed large flow of sector information for asset management team
