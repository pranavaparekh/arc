How we conduct a patent watch activity?

Setup

The setting up process involves a thorough understanding of the technology domain and formulating targeted search strategies on Thomson Innovation and Questel Orbit databases.

Monitor

The monitoring process involves developing a comprehensive taxonomy for the technology domain and bucketing the relevant patents into the respective taxonomy fields.

Deliver

The report is delivered in an excel format which is uploaded on our proprietary dashboard. The dashboard provides an easy way to analyze the big data on just a click of a button.
