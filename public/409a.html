409A valuations for pricing stock options have implications beyond taxation. CFOs at most successful start-ups know that an early stage non-compliant 409A report can create significant compliance challenges at a later date, delaying any M&A or IPO process. With over 4,000 valuation opinions under their belt, Aranca’s team of valuation experts with ASA, CFA and CPA credentials have the requisite experience to help you get through any stringent audit review process, including by the Big Four, SEC and IRS.
