What you can expect…

All documents submitted for edit will be checked for:

  • Business tone
  • Lexical choice and vocabulary
  • Right use of grammar (consistency of verbs/tenses/subject-verb agreement/punctuations, etc.)
  • Redundancy and repetition
  • Sentence structure, syntax and paragraph structure
  • Grammar and punctuation
  • Storyboarding (top-down approach, seamless blending of points, flow, etc.)
