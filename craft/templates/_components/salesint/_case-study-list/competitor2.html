Quarterly competitor profiling for a water and wastewater machinery manufacturer

A US-based company involved in design, development, and manufacturing water and wastewater treatment products/systems wanting to conduct quarterly assessment of its key competitors

The Challenge

  • Keeping a regular track on the developments and financial performance of client’s 10 key competitors in the global market to strategically position itself in the industry

Aranca Solution

  • Analysing 10 key competitors and identifying their segment which offer products and services similar to the client
  • Operational and financial analysis of relevant segments was undertaken:
    • Quarterly and year-to-date financial performance analysis
    • Operational update: Contract wins, New products/services, New technologies/patents, Management changes, M&A/JV’s/divestitures, Geographic expansion, other developments, among others
    • Commentary on financial targets / goals for the next quarter or for future
Tracked operational and financial performance of 10 competitors on a quarterly basis
