Go to market strategy for a Malaysian agency to penetrate in Indian medical gloves market

A Malaysian agency that works towards promoting the country's rubber and rubber products across the world. The client engaged Aranca to suggest the actionable market entry strategies for Malaysian companies to increase exports of medical gloves to India.

The Challenge

  • Identification of consumption pattern of different types of gloves (i.e. surgical, examination and nitrile) in hospitals, medical and dental institutes, clinics across India
  • Understand the value chain dynamics and role of manufacturer and intermediaries (Super stockists, stockists, and retailers)
  • Assess the competitive landscape of the Indian glove manufacturing industry and glove import scenario
  • Understand the procurement practices and buying policies for medical gloves for major hospital chains
  • Provide an overview of the private sector’s requirements on registration and certification for medical gloves in India

Aranca Solution

  • Identified and shortlisted major cities, key target customers (i.e. major hospitals), and key partners (Intermediates)
  • Identified demand-supply gap by using the equation: Apparent Consumption = Production + Import – Export
  • Identified current unmet needs and demands for different type of gloves
  • Understood the buying behavior and process followed in purchasing particular type or brand of glove in major corporate hospital chains
  • Provided the actionable market entry strategies for Malaysian companies to increase exports of medical gloves to India, based upon primary and secondary research findings
Identified ~8 corporate hospital chains that can be targeted by the client.
12+ super-stockists were suggested as potential partners that could help the client establish pan India distribution network.

Sample Output

  • Detailed analysis of the demand supply gap unmet needs, and demand was mapped against current share of Malaysian gloves across the product type, resulting in identification of opportunities for the client.
