Database development of prospects for a leading Finnish textile service provider

A leading Finnish textile service company offering cost-efficient and environment-friendly solutions for cleanliness and interior design of facilities, corporate clothing and protection

The Challenge

  • Identification of prospect companies across five end-user sectors in select cities across India:
    • Auto Aftermarket (Servicing);
    • Private Healthcare Centres / Facilities;
    • Quick Service Restaurants / Fast-food;
    • Retail; and
    • Metals

Aranca Solution

  • Shortlisting Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities with substantial presence of companies in end-user sectors cities across India:
  • Database development comprising of the following details:
    • Company Name
    • Number of Employees
    • Annual Revenue
    • Industry Classification
    • Business Description
    • VAT (Specific Unit)
    • Corporate Identity Number
    • Contact Details
A database of 5,000+ establishments was across multiple cities in each of the five target sectors was developed
