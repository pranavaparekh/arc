Quarterly competitor profiling for a industrial & environmental machinery manufacturer

A US-based industrial and environmental machinery manufacturer wanting to conduct quarterly assessment of its key competitors

The Challenge

  • Understanding the financial performance, strategic initiatives to stay ahead of competition
  • It also involved analyzing the market trends for O&G, power and water industries
  • Analyzing company’s management changes, realignment actions, IoT initiatives, among others

Aranca Solution

  • Quarterly comparison of all the competitors on the basis of the selected financial and operational metrics
  • Following operational and financial analysis:
    • Key initiatives/actions taken by the competitor during the quarter
    • Implications of those initiatives/actions on the client’s business
    • Quarterly and year-to-date financial performance analysis
    • Mapping key regional and industry specific trends impacting financial performance
Tracked financial performance of 15+ competitors on a quarterly basis
