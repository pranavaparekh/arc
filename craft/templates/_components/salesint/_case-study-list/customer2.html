Customer satisfaction survey covering existing homogenizer users in India

A leading Europe-based manufacturer of process equipments

The Challenge

  • The client wanted to assess satisfaction levels of its existing customers for homogenizers in India, and also understand the criterion governing their purchasing decisions.

Aranca Solution

  • Detailed analysis with respect to the existing satisfaction levels on the following six key parameters: Service, Price, Quality, Reliability, Ease of maintenance and Total cost of ownership
  • Relative benchmarking of the end-user’s perception of competitor products
  • Understanding the relative importance of various purchasing criterion
  • Detailed assessment highlighting the critical areas of improvement and the way forward for the client’s sales & marketing team in India
70+ telephonic interviews with existing customers within Dairy, F&B, Pharmaceuticals and Chemicals industry

Sample Output

  • Benchmarking purchasing criteria – client v/s competitors
