Analytical Frameworks Used

Technology Landscape Analysis

Patent / non-patent literature survey to analyze filling trends, competition, key markets, emerging technologies/companies, key inventors, etc.

Patent Portfolio Analysis

Deep dive analysis of patent portfolio to assess strengths / weaknesses, licensing opportunities, abandonment candidates and position vis-a-vis technology domain (leader / lagging).

White Space Analysis

'Problem vs solution' analysis of technology domain to identify unmet problems, key challenges, technology gaps and patenting opportunities.

Portfolio Benchmarking

Comparison of patent portfolio vis-a-vis peers to analyze relative strengths or weakness, IP focus of key players and potential threats from main competitors.

Infringement Analysis

Identify infringers for the identified patents through a thorough 'Target Scouting' analysis and detailed EoU analysis.
