Cookies are small packets of information that are stored by your web browser. They are put on your web browser by the websites that you visit. These packets of information do not contain personal information about you but they do allow us to know if you visit the same website twice or if you visit related websites. Some EU nations require you to provide a form of consent before cookies are placed on your web browser unless the cookie is necessary for the website to work. There is no agreed standard for how companies comply with these laws.
